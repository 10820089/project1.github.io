My personal interests

Given that I am a Software Engineering Major, one of my key intrests is computer programming. I have been dabbling into programming since I was 13 years old, in fact starting with HTML as my first language. As I had the wonderful opportunity to gain an associates degree concurent to a highschool deploma, I was able to take CS1400 and CS1410 in high school. After my first programming class I knew that programming was a passion of mine and spend a large majority of my time learning new things about programming languages or programming pet projects on the side.

Another one of my interests is movies and TV. Another way of putting this is I am a really great binge watcher. I spend a large majority of my free time with a TV show or movie in the background while I work on homework, programming, or anything else for that matter. My favorite genre's being British films, Sci-fi, Comedy, and Rom-Coms. Definitely one of my favorite hobbies and past times.

The last of my top three intrests would be Mixology. Though I am only 19 years old, I do enjoy making mocktails and learning about how different drinks are made. I just find the techniques and tools used to be fasinating. I try to make it my goal to find one new [non-alcholic lol] drink to make.
